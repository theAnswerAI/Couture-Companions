# Breed Curation Standards

## Our Signature Collection

Companion Couture specializes in a carefully curated collection of exceptional companion dogs. Rather than offering a wide variety of breeds, we focus exclusively on a select few that embody our standards of beauty, temperament, intelligence, and companionship.

Our collection is intentionally limited to:

### Teacup Pomeranians

Elegant, expressive, and luxuriously coated companions with vibrant personalities in an exceptionally small package.

**Ideal Characteristics**

* Adult weight: approximately 3–5 pounds
* Plush, well-groomed coats
* Fox-like facial features
* Confident and affectionate temperament
* Balanced proportions and refined structure
* Excellent socialization and family compatibility

---

### Teacup Maltese

Graceful, affectionate companions recognized for their flowing white coats, gentle personalities, and timeless elegance.

**Ideal Characteristics**

* Adult weight: approximately 3–5 pounds
* Pure white, silky coat
* Dark expressive eyes
* Sweet, calm disposition
* Elegant movement
* Well-socialized and family-oriented

---

### Medium Pomskies

A striking blend of athleticism and beauty, offering the intelligence of the Siberian Husky with the charm and expressive features of the Pomeranian.

**Ideal Characteristics**

* Adult weight: approximately 20–35 pounds
* Plush double coat
* Blue, amber, brown, or bi-colored eyes
* Symmetrical facial markings
* Confident, intelligent personality
* Friendly with children and families
* Exceptional trainability

---

# AI Design Rules

When generating imagery or designing any Companion Couture website, advertisements, social media, or marketing materials:

The AI should ONLY display these three breeds:

* Teacup Pomeranians
* Teacup Maltese
* Medium Pomskies

Do not generate or display:

* Large dog breeds
* Guard dogs
* Sporting breeds
* Working breeds
* Hounds
* Bulldogs
* Mixed breeds outside of Pomskies
* Any other breed not listed above

---

# Photography Standards

Every dog featured throughout the brand should appear:

* Professionally groomed
* Exceptionally healthy
* Happy and confident
* Naturally posed
* Luxuriously photographed
* In upscale residential or lifestyle environments
* Wearing tasteful, minimal accessories when appropriate

Images should evoke the feeling of an editorial luxury fashion campaign rather than a traditional breeder or kennel website.

---

# Lifestyle Representation

Photography should depict companions enjoying sophisticated, family-centered lifestyles, including:

* Designer living rooms
* Luxury kitchens
* Elegant gardens
* Coastal homes
* Private estates
* Boutique hotels
* Luxury vehicles
* First-class travel experiences
* Upscale cafés and shopping districts
* Beautiful parks and walking paths

The focus should always remain on the emotional connection between the companion and its family.

---

# Visual Diversity

Marketing imagery should feature individuals and families from diverse backgrounds, reflecting an inclusive luxury lifestyle while maintaining a cohesive editorial aesthetic.

Include:

* Couples
* Families with children
* Young professionals
* Empty nesters
* Active retirees
* Entrepreneurs
* Luxury travelers

The dog should always remain the visual focal point, with people serving to reinforce the lifestyle and emotional narrative.

---

# Brand Positioning Rule

Companion Couture is not positioned as a general breeder.

It is a luxury companion placement boutique specializing exclusively in:

• Teacup Pomeranians
• Teacup Maltese
• Medium Pomskies

Every design, photograph, video, advertisement, and AI-generated asset should reinforce this carefully curated collection. Limiting the selection enhances exclusivity, strengthens brand recognition, and positions Companion Couture as the premier destination for these signature companion breeds.
